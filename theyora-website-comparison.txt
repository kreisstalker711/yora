================================================================
YORA WEBSITE COMPARISON
Live production site (theyora.com) vs. New rebuild (this project)
Prepared: 2026-07-12
================================================================

--------------------------------------------------------------
1. WHAT THE TWO SITES ARE
--------------------------------------------------------------

theyora.com (LIVE)
- Runs on Shopify (confirmed by /collections/ URL structure, "Default
  Title" variant language, cancellation-policy wording on subscriptions,
  and store-pickup messaging - all Shopify/Shopify-app conventions).
- Fully transactional: real payment processing, real accounts, real
  order fulfillment, connected to a subscription app (Shopify
  Recharge-style) and Shopify Markets for currency/region.
- Visual design: plain, generic neutral palette (beige/white/brown),
  no distinct visual identity.

THIS REBUILD (yora/ Next.js project)
- Built from scratch on Next.js 16 + Tailwind v4 + Firebase
  (Auth + Firestore).
- Payment intentionally excluded per project scope - checkout is a
  "demo checkout" that writes a real Firestore order but charges
  nothing.
- Visual design: custom professional design system (deep forest green
  + muted sage + gold palette, Fraunces/Inter Tight typography,
  restrained card surfaces) - a deliberate upgrade over both the old
  prototype and the live site's generic look.
- Real photography from the brand's own product shoot is used
  throughout (product galleries, blog, About page, category cards).

3. FEATURES WE ARE MISSING (prioritized)
--------------------------------------------------------------

HIGH PRIORITY (core e-commerce trust/conversion features):
1. Real payment gateway - out of scope by explicit project decision,
   but this is the single biggest functional gap vs. the live site.
2. Coupon / promo code field at checkout - live Shopify sites have
   this by default; ours has none.
3. Legal policy pages - Privacy Policy, Refund Policy, Shipping Policy,
   Terms of Service currently just redirect to the Contact page. A
   real e-commerce site needs actual policy content (this is also a
   trust/compliance issue, not just a nice-to-have).
4. Full certification badge set - live site shows 7 badges (Vegan,
   ISO, FDA, QRO, FSSAI, NBA, Halal); we only show ISO + FSSAI.

MEDIUM PRIORITY (discovery & reach):
5. Site-wide search - ours only searches within the Shop page;
   there's no search icon/box in the header reachable from any page.
6. Region/country + language selector - live site supports multiple
   regions (e.g. Canada, India) with associated currencies; we only
   toggle INR/USD with a static conversion rate.
7. Marketplace/retailer links (Amazon, Flipkart, Blinkit, JioMart) -
   the live site cross-promotes its presence on these platforms in
   the footer; we have none of this.
8. Social media links in the footer (Facebook, Instagram, YouTube,
   LinkedIn) - currently absent from our Footer component.

LOWER PRIORITY (polish/nice-to-have):
9. Store pickup option at checkout.
10. Persistent, account-wide wishlist - ours only exists as page-local
    state on the Home page and is lost on refresh; not available on
    Shop or product detail pages, not saved to Firestore.
11. Limited-time bundle promotions in the announcement bar (e.g. the
    live site's "Buy 2 x 250ml EVCO for ₹500").
12. Payment method badges (Visa/Mastercard/RuPay/etc.) - not
    applicable until a real payment gateway is added.
13. Real recurring billing behind "Subscribe & Save" - currently just
    a UI flag stored on the order, no actual scheduled re-charge.

--------------------------------------------------------------
4. WHAT WE HAVE THAT THE LIVE SITE DOESN'T
--------------------------------------------------------------

- A cohesive, custom professional design system (the live site is
  visually generic - plain beige/white/brown with no distinct
  identity).
- A wholesale/bulk-inquiry form connected to a real backend
  (not confirmed to exist on the live site).
- Real, curated on-brand photography deliberately placed across
  product galleries, the About page, and every blog article (the
  live site's blog/story content wasn't inspected in enough depth to
  confirm equivalent photo curation).
- A from-scratch, fully-owned codebase (Next.js/React) vs. a
  Shopify-templated storefront - meaning far more flexibility to
  extend, but also meaning the "batteries included" Shopify features
  (search, discounts, multi-region tax/shipping, apps ecosystem) all
  have to be rebuilt manually if wanted.

--------------------------------------------------------------
5. RECOMMENDATION
--------------------------------------------------------------

If the goal is to eventually replace theyora.com with this rebuild,
close the gaps in this order:
  1. Real payment gateway (Razorpay is the natural fit for an
     India-based INR/USD store) - unblocks everything downstream.
  2. Legal policy pages (Privacy/Refund/Shipping/Terms) - required
     for any real payment gateway's compliance checks anyway.
  3. Site-wide search + coupon codes - highest-impact conversion
     features that are currently easy gaps.
  4. Full certification badge set + social links + marketplace links -
     low-effort trust/reach additions, mostly footer/content work.
  5. Persistent wishlist, region/language selector, real subscription
     billing - larger effort, lower urgency; do after the above.
